Project Details

Termux : Terminal

A Terminal based Task handling system that will basically help you to create your task and many more things. The main purpose was to give a feel of Arch Linux's Terminal and make you familiar with the Terminal Proceedings.

Features:

  • Task management with due dates.
  • A Calm and a Minimal Interface
  • It has a CUI Calculator to perform calculations

A quick view of the interface.



This is a experimenting project for an upcoming terminal based environment call Termux. A Team of one member with few alpha and beta testers are handling and making it happen so that mainly CS students can have a feel of it and haha easily can impress anyone. The external timer is already out.


Note: This is just an Experimenting Project, So feel free to give any suggestions or anyother bugs.
